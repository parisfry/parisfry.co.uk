ABOUT

Paris Fry is a London-based multidisciplinary artist combining printmaking and creative coding.

Through this approach she aims to bridge the gap between analogue and computational design methods.

Her work is underpinned by an interest in rhythm, repetition, and pushing the boundaries of contemporary textile practice.
