MUSE

Muse explores a visual language inspired by the electronic music duo Bicep and the atmosphere of live music culture.

Through layered compositions, experimental typography, and abstract geometric forms, this project explores movement and repetition within a graphic context.

By focusing on digitally driven techniques I developed a series of patterns and visuals that were applied across posters, flyers, apparel, and promotional materials.

The outcomes of this project combine bold colour palettes, texture, and repetition to reflect the energetic nature of electronic music.
