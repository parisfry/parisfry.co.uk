Framework

Framework explores the visual language of industrial architecture, drawing inspiration from the structure and atmosphere of Printworks

Through print-based experimentation, I investigated pattern, repetition, layering, and texture to translate the rhythm and materiality of industrial spaces into abstract visual compositions.

Using lino print, screen print, collage, and hand paper-cut techniques, I developed a series of compositions that combine photographic elements with constructed geometric forms.

The outcomes explore how these visuals could function at a larger scale through spatial visualisations and installation-style applications.
